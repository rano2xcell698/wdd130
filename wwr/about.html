River Rush Rafting

River Rush delivers safe, sustainable, and thrilling river adventures for families and thrill-seekers. Our certified guides, modern equipment, and deep respect for the river ensure an unforgettable trip every time.

Our History

Founded in 1998, River Rush started as a small guided-trip company run by local river experts. Over the years we've grown while keeping safety and customer experience as our highest priorities. Today, we offer world-class rafting adventures across multiple destinations.
